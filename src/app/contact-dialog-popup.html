Feedback Form

In order to ensure that the new Borealis Metrics Reporting application is fully functional and beneficial to the Canadian research data community, we are asking Borealis member institutions to take a few moments to provide feedback about their experience using the new Borealis Metrics Report.

All responses are voluntary and can be anonymous. We ask you to provide your institution so we can adequately understand regional differences. If you have specific questions to which you would like a direct response from the Borealis team, please include your email address so we can be in touch. Feedback Form
