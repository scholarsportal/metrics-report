Introduction

Borealis, the Canadian Dataverse Repository, is a digital research data repository available to researchers at participating Canadian universities and research organizations with infrastructure hosted by the University of Toronto Libraries (UTL) in Ontario, Canada. The open-source Dataverse software is developed and maintained by the Institute of Quantitative Social Science (IQSS) at Harvard University, with the assistance of Dataverse community members from around the world.

Borealis is designed to provide Canadian researchers with a place to store, share, and publish their research data. It is also designed to allow researchers from around the world to discover, view, and download research data from Canadian researchers.
